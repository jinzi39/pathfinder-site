What we offer

Every student gets a plan built around them.

No cookie-cutter advice. We take the time to understand who you are before we help you figure out where you're going.

🗺️

School List Strategy

We build a balanced, personalized list of schools — reach, match, and safety — based on your goals, strengths, and what will actually make you happy.

✍️

Essay Coaching

Your application essays should sound like you. We help you find the story only you can tell, then polish it until it shines — without scrubbing away your voice.

📋

Application Review

We go through every section of your application — activities list, awards, supplementals — with fresh eyes and honest feedback before you submit.
